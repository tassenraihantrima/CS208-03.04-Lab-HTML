Dhaka, Bangladesh

Location

Dhaka is the capital city of Bangladesh, located on the banks of the Buriganga River in central Bangladesh.

About

Weather

It has a tropical climate with hot, humid summers and mild, dry winters. But nowadays, it's mostly hot, which is concerning.

Population

More than 20 million people live in Dhaka now, making it one of the most densely populated cities in the world.

Sports

Cricket is the most popular sport in Dhaka, followed by football and traditional kabaddi.

Things To Do

  • Visit Lalbagh Fort
  • Explore Ahsan Manzil
  • Shop at New Market or Bashundhara City Mall
  • Take a colorful rickshaw ride through the busy streets
  • Try street food like fuchka, chotpoti, and jhalmuri
